Village Hub Ecosystem

How Village Hubs & Full Branches Work Together

Village Hubs are not competition to full branches — they are the fuel. Every Hub feeds trained, engaged students directly into the full branch network.

🎓
Graduation Pipeline
When a Hub student reaches Yellow Labgown, the nearest full branch is auto-notified via the portal. Hub owner earns a referral bonus. Branch gets a pre-trained student. Win-win every time.
🏆
One National Leaderboard
Hub students and branch students compete on the same ranking board. A White Labgown from a barangay hub can outrank students from city branches. Pride has no address.
⚔️
BattleBot Saturday Circuit
Hub students compete in BattleBot rounds hosted at the nearest full branch. Branch gets the crowd and energy. Hub students get the competitive arena. Both win.
📦
Supply Chain Link
Hub owners order filament and materials through their paired full branch at cost. Branch earns a small margin. Hub gets fast local supply. No waiting for Main Office delivery.
🤝
Mentor Branch Pairing
Every Hub is officially paired with one full branch. Monthly check-ins, curriculum support, and first right of refusal on all graduating Green students from that Hub.
📣
Co-Branded Marketing
Hub and paired branch run co-branded local posts. Hub owners are the most trusted voices in their barangay — their word-of-mouth feeds enrollment to both.
🎉
Promote-Up Ceremony
When a student graduates from Green to Yellow, both the Hub and the full branch celebrate it together. Photos. Social media. The moment becomes marketing for the entire network.
📈
Hub-to-Branch Upgrade Path
A successful Hub owner who maxes out demand can upgrade to a full branch. Their ₱199,000 Hub fee is credited toward the full branch investment. Best operators stay and grow.
🏘️
Always Refilling
Every student that graduates to a full branch opens a slot for a new White Labgown beginner at the Hub. The Village Hub is the permanent entry point of the entire 3DBotics® ecosystem — it never runs out of students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do I need a tech background to own a 3DBotics® branch?▼
No tech background required. 3DBotics® provides complete 2-day face-to-face training for you and your facilitator before you open. Many of our most successful franchisees come from business, teaching, or retail backgrounds — not tech.
How many students do I need to break even monthly?▼
With the minimum tuition of ₱3,995/student and typical overhead (rent, salary, fees), most branches break even with 15–20 active students per month. Most branches exceed this within their first 3 months.
Is the franchise fee inclusive of everything I need?▼
Yes — both packages are all-in. Equipment, tools, laptop, curriculum, training, marketing materials, and AI-Platform access are all included. You provide the space (rent), a facilitator (salary), and utilities. No surprise costs.
Can I set my own tuition rates?▼
Yes, but only above the minimum floor rate of ₱3,995/month per student. You may charge more based on your local market. You may never charge below ₱3,995 — this protects the brand's value across all branches.
Are there royalty fees or percentage-based charges?▼
No. 3DBotics® does not collect royalties or take a percentage of your revenue. Your only monthly obligations are ₱495/active student (AI Platform fee) and ₱2,000 flat (brand license). Everything else is yours to keep.
What is the franchise term and what are the renewal terms?▼
The franchise term is 3 years. Renewal is available at 30% of your original franchise cost — so if you took Option 2 (₱660,000), renewal is ₱198,000. Your city exclusivity and brand rights continue.
What if my city is already taken?▼
If your primary city is taken by a Master Franchisee, you may still open within the city — but must be located outside the 8km radius from the existing branch. In this case, a royalty fee to the Master Franchisee applies, the details of which will be discussed during your discovery call.
What age group does TechDojo cater to?▼
TechDojo serves three groups: Kids (ages 6–12), Teens (ages 13–19), and Adults (ages 20 and above). Flexible schedules allow all age groups to co-exist in the same branch with different time slots.
Do I need to hire a teacher with a tech degree?▼
No. Your facilitator does not need a tech or engineering degree. They just need to be teachable and passionate. 3DBotics® trains your facilitator through our included 2-day face-to-face training before opening, plus ongoing weekly Zoom coaching.
